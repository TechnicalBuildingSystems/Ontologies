Abstract

The ontology formally represents human experiencse about IEQ factors, such as thermal comfort, visual comfort , acoustic comfort and IAQ (Indoor Air Quality). It can intregate with time ontology and bim4hex.

IRI: https://w3id.org/ibp/hbc/hex#AcousticComfort

A concept of IEQ factor representing acoustic comfort. It can be described by the combination of quality and acoustic sensation.

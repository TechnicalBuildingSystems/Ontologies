Abstract

The Moore Machine Ontology is an ontology to explicitly describe control logic compliant to the formalism specified by Moore (Lunze, 2009). The ontology and its use will be documented in a conference paper. The ontology is implemented using the free protege tool (protege.stanford.edu) (Musen, 2015). M. A. Musen (2015): The protege project: a look back and a look forward. AI matters, 1(4):4-12, doi: 10.1145/2757001.2757003. J. Lunze (2009): Ereignisdiskrete Systeme: Modellierung und Analyse dynamischer Systeme mit Automaten, Markovketten und Petrinetzen. Oldenbourg, Munich, Germany.
